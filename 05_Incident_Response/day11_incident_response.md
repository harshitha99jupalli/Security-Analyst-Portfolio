## What is Incident Response?
Incident Response is the process companies use to handle
security attacks like hacking, malware, or phishing.

The goal is to stop the attack, fix the problem, and prevent it
from happening again.

## Why Incident Response is important
- Protects company data
- Reduces damage
- Restores systems quickly
- Builds customer trust

## Incident Response Lifecycle

### 1. Preparation
Getting tools, training staff, and making plans before an attack.

### 2. Identification
Finding and confirming that a security incident happened.

### 3. Containment
Stopping the attack from spreading.

### 4. Eradication
Removing malware and fixing vulnerabilities.

### 5. Recovery
Restoring systems and monitoring them closely.

### 6. Lessons Learned
Reviewing what happened and improving security.

## Example Scenario
A user reports a phishing email.

- Identify: Confirm it is phishing
- Contain: Block sender
- Eradicate: Remove email
- Recover: Reset password
- Learn: Improve email filters
